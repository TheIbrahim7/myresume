ABOUT

I am a coding boot camp graduate who loves to code, problem-solve and create. My non-traditional coding background includes: a degree in Advertising, teaching English abroad, teaching second grade in Dallas, and currently learning to code on my own. Coding came into my life when I created a website to teach others how to learn Korean. Little did I know, I would be learning a new language myself in order to optimize my website. I used my knowledge of advertising, graphic design, social media, and pure grit to accumulate more than 10K followers in less than a year. However, coding was a skill that I still had a yearning to improve upon. That’s when I joined Coding Dojo Bootcamp to get a formal education on the ins and outs of coding. I learned a full-stack, collaborated with others, and built projects all while holding a full-time job as a teacher during the pandemic. As the boot camp has come to an end, I’m continuing to improve my skills by building projects using different technologies. I also have skills in other fields like branding, icon design or web development.
